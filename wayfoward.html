W – Way Forward

To improve, I plan to:

  • Pause and breathe before speaking.
  • Write down my responses during conversations to avoid interrupting.
  • Practice curiosity by truly listening and waiting for others to finish.
  • Accept that overachieving is not always helpful for teamwork — and that joy, not perfection, is the real goal.

The article on turn-taking shows that interruptions are not always negative — they can express support or shared understanding — but they must be balanced. Without care, they can create tension or disrupt group dynamics (Lestary et al., 2017). Similarly, Courtright et al. (2017) highlight how unchecked individual dominance in teams often leads to lower group satisfaction and performance.

Starting this Monday, I will share my personal goal with the team. I will keep a daily journal, and each week I will ask my teammates to fill out a short tracker to give me feedback. This will help me stay aware, adjust when needed, and reflect on my progress over time.
