Expectation 'paper'

Welcome to this website—rather than writing a paper, I chose to create a website for a few reasons.

First, I wanted to visualise my learning journey. The GROW and I-WE-IT frameworks inspired me, and I combined them into one path. I used the order Reality, Obstacles, I, Way Forward, We, It, and Goals because it reflects how I learn: starting from what is, facing challenges, reflecting, taking action, and involving others—always with goals in mind. I drew a mountain in Photoshop to show this path. By clicking on the steps, you can explore each part.

Second, the website lets me integrate feedback and track progress. In the bottom left corner, there’s a button called ‘Help me track progress’ where teammates from the Expert Team, the MT Team, and others can give feedback. I’ll connect this to a live results section next week. I’ll also share it during our Monday meeting.

Finally, this was a chance to use and improve my web development skills. I built the site using HTML and Visual Studio Code with Live Preview. I used ChatGPT to speed up coding and host everything on GitHub.

I hope this gives a clear and engaging view of my journey.
