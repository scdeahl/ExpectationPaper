Growth Journey Explained

Welcome to this website.

I created it because I’m a very visual person, and I wanted to approach this serious assignment in a playful and engaging way. Designing a website gave me the freedom to shape the story creatively and make the process of reflection feel more alive.

I chose the mountain as a metaphor because personal growth is a journey. The mountain helps me visualise that path: where I begin, what I face along the way, and what I’m reaching for.

The order of the steps follows the logic of real change. I start with Reality, because I need to know where I stand. Then I face the Obstacles that make progress difficult. From there, I reflect on I — what’s going on inside me and what I want to change. That leads to a Way Forward with practical steps. Once that’s in place, I can shift focus to WE — how I contribute to the team — and together we build the IT, our shared result. At the top are my Goals, the bigger picture I’m climbing toward. This structure helps me show how growth moves from self-awareness to shared outcomes, step by step.

Click on the words in the mountain to explore the steps of my journey.
